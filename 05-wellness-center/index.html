What We Offer

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

We don't do infinity pools or juice bars. We do clean equipment, good AC, and enough space for everyone.

🏋️

Free Weights Area

Dumbbells from 2.5 to 50 kg, barbells, benches, squat racks, and deadlift platforms. Properly maintained, always available.

🏃

Cardio Zone

Treadmills, ellipticals, stationary bikes, and rowing machines. TV screens to keep you entertained during those long sessions.

❄️

Air Conditioned

Full AC throughout the gym — because Pune summers are no joke. Comfortable temperature year-round so you can focus on the workout.

🔒

Locker Rooms

Separate men's and women's changing rooms with secure lockers, mirrors, and clean washrooms. Maintained twice daily.

🅿️

Free Parking

Dedicated two-wheeler and four-wheeler parking right outside the gym. No circling the block looking for a spot.

💪

Machine Section

Cable machines, leg press, lat pulldown, chest press, and more. All from reputable brands, serviced regularly.
