Why IronEdge

This Isn't a Gym. It's a Proving Ground.

We opened IronEdge in 2021 with one belief: fitness should be earned, not entertained. No air-conditioned treadmill rows. No mirrors for flexing. Just chalk, barbells, and a community that holds you accountable.

Our coaches are certified CrossFit Level 2 trainers, competitive athletes, and flat-out obsessed with your progress. Every WOD is programmed with intent — strength, skill, conditioning, all balanced across weekly cycles.

  • 6,000 sq ft industrial training floor with rigs, ropes & turf
  • 8 certified coaches — CF-L2, USAW, & Precision Nutrition
  • Open gym access 5:30 AM – 9 PM for programmed athletes
  • Monthly in-house competitions & quarterly throwdowns
  • Free body composition analysis every 8 weeks
